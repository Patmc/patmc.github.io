Vision Carpentry are bespoke fitted furniture and cabinet makers. We specialise in custom built wardrobes, TV and audio units, home offices, kitchens and bathrooms.

What We Offer

  • Bedrooms and Wardrobes
  • Kitchens
  • Bathrooms
  • TV and Audio/Media Units
  • Home Office/Library
  • Shelving and Storage Solutions
  • Refurbishments and Extensions

With our wide range of experience and knowledge, we are happy to design, build and fit any wood products that a client may desire.

Kitchens

Vision Carpentry design, plan and fit kitchens. We don't just install the units, worktops and appliances. We manage every aspect of the project including plumbing, electrics, tiling and structural work if necessary.

Bathrooms

We also design, plan and install bathrooms. We specialise in luxury wet rooms using the latest wet room materials from market leader Fermacell.

10 Years Experience

Vision Carpentry has worked extensively in London and Belfast for the past 10 years. We work primarily on residential projects, taking on a variety of work directly from private clients as well as architects and designers.

We deliver on value by designing products that function properly and are finished to the highest standard using quality woods and wood veneers. We only use the highest quality ironmongery from suppliers such as Hafele and Blum.
